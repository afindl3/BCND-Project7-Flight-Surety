Buy flight insurance:

You can purchase up to 1 ether worth of flight insurance. In the event that your flight is delayed, you may collect a payout of 1.5X your payment. Once you have bought insurance for a specific flight, you may not increase or decrease the amount of insurance, or receive a refund for the purchase. You may purchase insurance for multiple flights.
